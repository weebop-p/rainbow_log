Name: Stuffed Bell Peppers
Ingredients: bell peppers, rice, carrots, leeks, mushroom, herbs (thyme, rosemary), salt, pepper, white wine, olive oil, [optional: cooked chicken]
Recipe: 
1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. Cook rice according to package instructions and set aside.
3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil and sauté mixed vegetables until softened.
4. Add a splash of white wine and allow it to reduce slightly.
5. Stir in the cooked rice, herbs, salt, and pepper. Mix well. If using chicken, fold it into the mixture.
6. Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove the seeds.
7. Stuff each bell pepper with the rice and vegetable mixture.
8. Place the stuffed peppers in a baking dish, and add a bit of water to the bottom of the dish.
9. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the bell peppers are tender.
10. Serve hot with a drizzle of olive oil or sauce of your choice.
