Name: Baked Oat-Hazelnut-Chocolate Cereal Bars
Ingredients: Old-fashioned rolled oats, Hazelnuts, Chocolate chips or chunks, Honey or maple syrup, Coconut oil or unsalted butter, Vanilla extract, Optional: almonds, puffed rice cereal (adjust wet ingredients accordingly)
Recipe: 
1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking pan with parchment paper.

2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the oats (1 cup), hazelnuts (1/2 cup), chocolate chips or chunks (3/4 cup), and optional ingredients if using.

3. In a separate bowl, mix together the honey or maple syrup (1/2 cup), melted coconut oil or butter (1/4 cup), and vanilla extract (1 tsp).

4. Pour the wet mixture into the dry ingredients and stir until everything is well-coated.

5. Press the mixture firmly into the prepared baking pan.
6. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until the edges are golden brown.
7. Allow the bars to cool completely before cutting into squares.
Note: If you're adding extra dry ingredients, ensure to adjust the wet ingredients so the mixture binds well.
