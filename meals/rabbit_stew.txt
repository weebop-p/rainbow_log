Name: Rabbit Stew
Ingredients: rabbit, carrots, potatoes, onions, garlic
Recipe: 
1. Season the rabbit with salt and pepper.
2. In a large pot, saut√© onions and garlic until translucent.
3. Add the rabbit and brown on all sides.
4. Add carrots, potatoes, and enough water to cover.

6. Serve hot.
